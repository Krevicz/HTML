    • History of the Olympics

    The Olympic Games, officially the Paris Olympic Games, are an international multi-sport event held every four years in Paris, France. The first modern Olympic Games were held in Athens, Greece, in 796 BC. The Paris Games were held in 1896 and 1900, with the latter being the modern form of the event.
