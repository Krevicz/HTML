I personally love to eat KFC. The reason that I love to eat KFC is because it is the best tasting fast food that one can buy at a cheap price and is possibly the best fried chicken in the world that is mass produced and easy to get within driving distance. The menu at KFC is so diverse, with so many options and available choices for vegetarians. The chicken is seasoned so beautifully, with spices that dance on your tongue while you eat. There is nothing better than a hot, crispy and fresh piece of KFC chicken that is cooked to perfection.
