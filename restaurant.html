Welcome to Zac's Restaurant

Zac's Restaurant is a family-owned restaurant that serves delicious Afghan food. We are located in Bella Vista, Sydney. We have been serving our customers for over 10 years. We are known for our delicious food and excellent customer service. Our menu includes a variety of Afghan dishes, including kebabs, rice dishes, and vegetarian options. We also offer catering services for special events. Come visit us and enjoy a delicious meal in a warm and welcoming atmosphere.
